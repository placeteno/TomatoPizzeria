Our Story

From a small town in Tuscany...

Our family immigrated from Italy and settled in Milwaukee in 1930. Carrying only a suitcase and less than $50 in their pocket, our grandparents knew they wanted to prosper and create a better future for their family. Tomato Pizzeria was founded to fullfill that dream and, 10 years later, we continue to provide the best services and food to our neighbors.
